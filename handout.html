⚠️ 4 Hazard Categories

🔴 Mechanical

  • Rotating spindles, chucks, shafts
  • Sharp cutting tools & edges
  • Hot chips (>500°C) & grinding debris

🟠 Chemical / Fire

  • Cutting fluids — skin & slip hazard
  • Metal dust — combustion risk
  • SDS sheets must be accessible

🟡 Electrical

  • Damaged cords or plugs
  • Coolant near electrical panels
  • Use LOTO for all electrical work

🔵 Ergonomic

  • Repetitive gripping & twisting
  • Heavy lifting — get help for >50 lbs
  • Long standing — use anti-fatigue mats

🚨 Emergency Procedures

🔴 Machine Hazard

  1. Hit E-stop immediately
  2. Clear the area
  3. Notify supervisor
  4. Do not restart machine

🚑 Injury / Medical

  1. Stop machine, call for help
  2. Do not move injured person
  3. Send someone to meet EMS
  4. Preserve the scene

🔥 Fire

  1. Sound the fire alarm
  2. Evacuate via nearest exit
  3. Use PASS technique if trained
  4. Report to muster point
